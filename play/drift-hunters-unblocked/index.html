How to Play

Controls

  • Arrow Keys or WASD - Control car movement
  • Space Bar - Handbrake
  • Shift - Manual gear shifting
  • C - Change camera view
  • R - Reset car position

Drifting Techniques

  1. Initiation: Begin your drift by using one of these methods:
    • Handbrake entry
    • Clutch kick
    • Power over
    • Feint drift
  2. Angle Control: Maintain the right drift angle using:
    • Counter-steering
    • Throttle control
    • Weight transfer
  3. Chain Drifts: Link multiple corners together for higher scores

Game Modes

  • Free Roam: Practice your drifting skills without pressure
  • Score Attack: Earn points through stylish drifts and combinations
  • Time Attack: Complete tracks as quickly as possible while drifting

  1. Start with Beginner-Friendly Cars: The Toyota AE86 and Nissan Silvia are great for learning
